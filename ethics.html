IT-Related Ethics

As technology evolves, it's crucial for professionals to follow ethical principles to ensure safety, fairness, and accountability.

Guidelines for Developers

Developers should follow principles like honesty, privacy protection, and non-maleficence. The ACM Code of Ethics provides detailed guidance: ACM Code of Ethics.

Ethical Considerations for Data Scientists

Data scientists must consider issues such as bias, transparency, and consent. According to the Data Science Association, ethical standards include being truthful about data limitations and avoiding harmful outcomes (Data Science Association Code of Conduct).
